About Me

Hello! My name is Aisata Ba. I am a 20-year-old college student at La Salle University, majoring in Digital Arts and minoring in English. I love using my art to tell stories and to express what words cannot. I also enjoy experimenting with videography and animation. A long-term goal of mine is to direct my own film someday!

Don’t mean to toot my own horn, but I’ve done a lot of things--and I’m just getting started!
